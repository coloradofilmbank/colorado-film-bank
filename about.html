About Colorado Film Bank

Our Mission

Colorado Film Bank preserves, digitizes, catalogs, and provides access to Colorado's visual, audio, and documentary history. Our mission is to safeguard historical media before it is lost to time and make it accessible to researchers, museums, libraries, filmmakers, educators, and the public.

Who We Are

Founded by archivist and filmmaker Ashley Swendsen, Colorado Film Bank has spent 4 decades preserving historical media and creating digital archives throughout Colorado and beyond.

Colorado Film Bank specializes in film preservation, audiovisual restoration, oral histories, metadata creation, database development, archival assessment, historical research, and documentary production.

Preservation Expertise

Colorado Film Bank has provided preservation, digitization, consulting, and archival services for museums, libraries, filmmakers, educational institutions, and historical organizations throughout the United States.

  • United States Air Force Academy Special Collections
  • Pro Rodeo Hall of Fame
  • Canon City Prison Museum
  • Space Foundation Museum
  • Western Museum of Mining and Industry
  • Manitou Springs Heritage Center
  • Ute Pass Historical Society
  • Pikes Peak International Hill Climb Collections
  • National Prisoner of War Digital Collection
  • St. Augustine Native American Boarding School Archives

Our Archive

Colorado Film Bank maintains one of the largest privately preserved multimedia archives in Colorado, containing films, photographs, audio recordings, oral histories, documents, and historical media relating to Colorado history and the American West.

The archive includes preservation equipment for film, videotape, audio tape, vinyl recordings, slides, photographs, and rare legacy media formats.

Documentary & Research Work

Colorado Film Bank materials and research have been used by documentary filmmakers, historians, museums, publishers, television productions, and major motion pictures. Research and archival contributions have supported projects involving Colorado history, military history, transportation, racing, Native American history, aviation, and western heritage.
